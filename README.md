# 🌱 DD's Garden

My digital garden — a public space where ideas grow in the open.

Unlike a blog, nothing here is ever "finished." Notes move through stages of growth:

| Stage | Folder | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 🌰 Seed | `🌰 Seeds` | Raw ideas, fleeting thoughts, things to explore |
| 🌱 Seedling | `🌱 Seedlings` | Ideas starting to develop — rough notes taking shape |
| 🌿 Sapling | `🌿 Saplings` | Substantial drafts with real structure |
| 🌳 Evergreen | `🌳 Evergreen` | Mature, polished pieces I stand behind |

Written in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md), synced to GitHub.
